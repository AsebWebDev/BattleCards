I N S T R U C T I O N S

It is a two player game. You got to share your mouse and switch players. The current player's scoreboard will be highlighted. How to play?

Each round has three phases :

  1. Who is first? --- You roll the dice to decide who picks the first card.
  2. Create your little army! --- Each player has to pick up three cards. You switch turns after picking one.
  3. Time for Battle! --- Each player picks one card for battle. The current player chooses one of the character traits to attack with. Then both players throw the dice to add some luck to their score.

INSTRUCTIONS

You are not anonymous. Change your name and click START to begin the game.
